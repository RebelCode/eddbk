Bookings for Easy Digital Downloads is a premium, third-party extension for Easy Digital Downloads, brought to you by RebelCode.

To display the booking wizard to your clients, add the [eddbk] shortcode to any post, page or other area on your site.
To show the booking wizard for a single service only, add its ID to the shortcode: [eddbk service="1543"]. Learn more here.
